What SocialKYC is

SocialKYC allows you to create, manage, and use social credentials. These credentials verify your control over social accounts (Twitter, Discord, GitHub, Twitch, etc.), email addresses, or phone numbers.

  • Verify your digital identity
  • Receive a social credential
  • Store credentials in your wallet
  • Save proof of validity on the KILT blockchain
  • Use credentials to log into services

How SocialKYC Works

Users must connect their wallet with SocialKYC before starting. Currently, only the Sporran wallet is supported. The verification process includes:

  • Email verification via confirmation link
  • Twitter verification via public post
  • Discord, GitHub, Twitch, Telegram, and YouTube verification via login and permission grants

Once verified, a credential is issued, and an attestation hash is anchored to the KILT blockchain.
